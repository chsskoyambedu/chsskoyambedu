About the School

CHSS Koyambedu Started in the year 1935, Tamil is the primary medium of instruction here with student teacher ratio is 22:1. The school has been able to produce exceptional results in the yester year secondary and higher secondary public examinations because of the fervent efforts put in by the thirty two teachers and especially that of the School headmistress Mrs. Mrunalini.
Over the years the school has transformed itself into a model corporation school in the city by acquiring 100% Result for consecutive years.The school has a very strong inclination towards non-academic activities in Sports, Arts and other extra-curricular events happening in and around the city.

Highlights

Results speaks louder than words! With the best of available resources the school strives to provide quality education to build strong cohorts of students independent and capable enough to handle life situation. Some of the Key Highlights at our School are:

  • Smart Classroom
  • Experienced Educators
  • Inclusive Learning Environment
  • Computer Labs
  • Exposure to Events & Training
  • Life Skill Training

Located amidst the Busiest Bus Station and Perishable commodity market the School stands out to provide a learning environment inclusive enough for students from all walks of life to realise their true potential.
